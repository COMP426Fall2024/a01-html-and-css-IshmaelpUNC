Odin's family includes his wife Frigg, who is known as the queen of Asgard and the goddess of foresight and wisdom. Their children include Baldr, the god of light and purity, Hodr, who is associated with darkness and winter. Odin's other known child is Bragi, the god of poetry and music, and Thor, the powerful god of thunder and protector of Asgard.
